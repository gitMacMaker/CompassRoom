The room

Twelve tables, one compass.

The Compass Room seats forty on a good night. The menu is short on purpose — a kitchen that does five things beautifully will always beat one that does fifty adequately.

Fish comes off the dock, not off a truck. The wine list fits on one page. And the pace is set by the tide, not the turn time.
